We are excited to announce that CodeshiftCommunityBot, a new Github App, _is coming soon_!
This app will automatically run Codeshift codemods in your repository to keep your dependencies up to date.

With CodeshiftCommunityBot, you no longer have to worry about manually running codemods or struggling to keep your dependencies up to date.
The app will take care of these tasks for you, freeing up your time to focus on more important things.

Stay tuned for more information about CodeshiftCommunityBot and how it can help you maintain your repository.
We can't wait to share this new tool with you!
